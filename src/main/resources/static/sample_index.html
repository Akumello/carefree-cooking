Mexican Rice

Authentic mexican rice with onions and bell pepper

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Chicken boulion

Method

  1. Preheat pan on medium with a small amount of neutral oil to coat the pan.
  2. Add rice, and fry for a few minutes. Stirring constantly until some rice becomes lightly browned.
  3. Blend tomatoes, garlic, onion, chicken boullion, salt with a small amount of water.
  4. Pour blended mixture into rice, and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the rest of the water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce to low and cover for 20 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve!
